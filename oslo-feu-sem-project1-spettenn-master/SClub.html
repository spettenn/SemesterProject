What is the Science Club?

Join our Science youth team to help us at the Museum conduct experiments and learn about science in a fun way. Here at the Science museum we take every procation to make learning as fun as possible. We have special Exhibitions and lectures online for the members of the club, and we also send out weekly news in science and technlogoy.

How do i become a ember?

All you have to do to become a member is to sign up bellow on our form. After you have signed up you wil recieve an Email confirmation that everything is in order. you will be enrolled in our weekly news-letters and you will be added to our online exhibition.
