What can you do?

If COVID-19 is spreading in your community, stay safe by taking some simple precautions, such as physical distancing, wearing a mask, keeping rooms well ventilated, avoiding crowds, cleaning your hands, and coughing into a bent elbow or tissue. Check local advice where you live and work.

What can we do?

In the beggining of this Pandemic, the staff at Sandvika Science Museum ask ourselves "What could we do?". We all elaborated and we came to the conclusion that our task will not change form what we usually do. As educators we still look at our task of informing the public with correct and scientific based information as a key role in the fight against COVID-19. This is why we hold online Exhibitions to reach our audiance and continue our quest for knowledge.
